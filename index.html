We are Good Game Network

Good Game Network is a platform for gamers. a matchmaking service for DotA. Good game network will allow teams to play against each other and earn money playing.

Matchmake

Teams will choose how much money they invest in a single game, select their opponent from out matchmaking tool and start the game.

Earn

After the game the money is transferred to the winner and the teams will see a detailed statistics. Teams will rate and endorse each other!
